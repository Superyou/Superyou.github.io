I'm working on the security topic related to hardware designs. Side channel attacks and Oblivious RAM are the 2 most major topics. For the side channel attack topic, I am interested in defending specific popular side channel attacks using optimal algorithms on specific state-of-art hardware ( e.g. intel SGX ). For the Oblivious RAM topic, I am thinking about optimizing nowaday's oblivious RAM system and dealing with the trade-off between security and overheads.

Previously, I did some research on accelerating and implementing binarized nueral network in specific problem when I was an undergraduate, so I am also familiar with neural network problems.
